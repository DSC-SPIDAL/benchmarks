Meetings¶

The project members of SPIDAL conduct biweekly online meetings which are recorded by IU’s video bridge and viewed with Pexip technology. Each week features a different presenter to discuss their current work. These videos are included below along with corresponding slides from the presentation. The initial presentation in December 2014 was updated with video in June 2015.
